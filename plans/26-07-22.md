# Wednesday 7/22 — 60 min

Building on our fundamentals base. Today we add an **offensive down-screen** action
and put our team defense to the test with **3v3**. Keep the movement high and the
lines short — everyone stays busy.

| Time | Block | Detail | Court | Purpose |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 5 min | Roll call + roundtable | Take **roll call**, then quick circle-up — one thing each athlete wants to work on today. | Sideline / No basket | Set the tone, build team identity |
| 5 min | Dynamic warm-up | Light jog, high knees, butt kicks, side shuffles, arm circles. Finish with a few sprints. | Full court | Get loose, set the pace |
| 5 min | Dribbling (warm-up) | Between the legs, Under drags — down and back, small lines. | Full court | Baseline ball-handling |
| 10 min | Layup lines | Right hand and left hand layup lines. Emphasize finishing high off the glass. | Half court | Finishing at the rim, both hands |
| 5 min | Defense — individual | Closeout drill + 1v1 coverage, wing to wing. Chop steps, high hands. | Half court | Reinforce stance and effort |
| 10 min | Offense — setting a down-screen | Weak-side (away from the ball) player sets a **down-screen** for a teammate, who uses it to pop out to the perimeter for a catch; screener and cutter switch spots. Teach the screening angle, a wide/legal base, and the cutter setting up their man before using the screen. Run both sides. | Half court | Learn to free a teammate with an off-ball screen |
| 10 min | Defense — team 3v3 | Live 3v3 half court. Defense must talk through the down-screen — call it out and decide to **switch** or **fight over**. Off-ball defenders pack the paint and sprint to contest on the catch. | Half court | Apply help + screen defense against real actions |
| 10 min | Scrimmage | Short, fun 5v5 — let them play and look for the down-screen action live. **Limitation:** everyone on the offense must touch the ball at least once before the team can score. | Half court | Play the game, keep it fun; force ball movement and involve everyone |
