# Wednesday 6/24 — 60 min

Our **first practice** of the season. The goal today is to meet each other, set the
tone, and get a baseline on fundamentals (dribbling, layups, defense) with plenty
of movement and fun. Keep it light — build confidence and a love for the game.

| Time | Block | Detail | Court | Purpose |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 10 min | Roundtable introductions | Quick circle-up — everyone says their name, grade, favorite player, and one thing they want to get better at this season. Coaches go first to set the tone. | Sideline / No basket | Meet each other, build comfort and team identity |
| 5 min | Dynamic warm-up | Light jog, high knees, butt kicks, side shuffles, arm circles. Finish with a few sprints. | Full court | Get loose, set the pace |
| 5 min | Dribbling (warm-up) | Between the legs, Under drags — down and back, small lines. | Full court | Baseline ball-handling |
| 10 min | Layup lines | Right hand and left hand layup lines. Emphasize finishing high off the glass. | Half court | Finishing at the rim, both hands |
| 5 min | Defense — individual | Closeout drill + 1v1 coverage, wing to wing. Chop steps, high hands. | Half court | Intro to defensive stance and effort |
| 10 min | Defense — team (Touch-the-key) | On every pass, off-ball defenders go back and touch the key with a foot before moving freely; only the on-ball defender stays out. Sprint to contest when your man gets the ball, drop back to touch the paint on the next pass. | Half court | Pack-the-paint help instincts |
| 10 min | Offense — 5-out spacing | All five spaced around the perimeter, no one in the post. Stay away from the ball carrier; re-space on every pass/drive. Add cut-and-switch (screen-away) and corner drive-and-kick as they get it. | Half court | Spread the floor, stop bunching around the ball |
| 5 min | Scrimmage | Short, fun 3v3 or 5v5 — let them play and apply today's reps. | Half court | Play the game, keep it fun |
